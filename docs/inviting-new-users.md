# Inviting New Users

# Inviting Colleagues and Friends to pCloudy Unified Platform

## Step 1: Log In

1. Go to [pCloudy Unified Login](#).
2. Log in with your credentials.

## Step 2: Initiate Invitation

Click the "Invite" icon on the top right of the Trial page.

## Step 3: Fill Invitation Details

1. In the pop-up:
   - Enter the **Project Name**.
   - Add colleagues' **Email IDs** (comma-separated).

2. Click "Send Invite" to generate invitation links.

## Step 4: Share Links

1. Invited users receive an email with the invitation link.  
2. Users click the link to register and access the pCloudy Unified Platform.
